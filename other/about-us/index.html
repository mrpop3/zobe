About Zobe.com

At Zobe.com our mission is to being you the most innovative and fun chat experience on the web.

You can read the rest of this page or you can take a few moments to log in and discover it for yourself. It’s free and requires no registration to get started.

Zobe has an innovative karma system that improves upon the traditional chat experience by building in social incentives and extra capabilities to enhance the chat experience for valued members and to reduce the burden of chat room trolls.

We’re a small group of web professionals with a combined decades of experience in building fast, efficient and easy to use web applications.

Zobe is powered by HTML5 and JavaScript on the client side, Go and MySQL on the server side.

If web application development or marketing are your passion, drop us an email. We’re always looking for passionate people to work with.
